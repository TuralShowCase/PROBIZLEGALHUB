Real Estate

A simple legal gap can put millions in property investments at risk. PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC provides full corporate protection in the purchase and sale of commercial real estate, legal support for large-scale construction projects, and resolution of complex property disputes (title deed problems).

Reliable Legal Guarantee for Million-Manat Investments

The real estate and construction sector in Azerbaijan is one of the most profitable but also one of the highest legal-risk sectors. Imprecisely drafted investment contracts, handovers with encumbered documentation, and failure to obtain necessary state permits in a timely manner can cause millions in losses.

We do not simply formalise purchases and sales; we analyse the full legal transparency of a property before large-scale investment, overcome bureaucratic barriers for uninterrupted construction, and provide legal guarantees for your project's realisation.

What Do We Resolve in the Property & Construction Sector?

  • Commercial Property Audit — Before purchasing or leasing, we conduct an in-depth legal investigation of freezes, hidden encumbrances (undisclosed mortgages), third-party rights, and tax risks. We guarantee risk-free transactions.
  • Drafting Complex Sale & Purchase Agreements — Specific contracts for hotels, factories, car parks, agricultural land, and residential complexes where economic risks are correctly distributed between the parties.
  • Construction Law & Contract Disputes — Construction permit disputes, bill of quantities disputes, arbitration and court disputes over Form-2/Form-3 completion and payment delays between main contractor and subcontractors.
  • Title Deed & Registry Discrepancies — Administrative court annulment of refusals by the Real Estate Services Agency to issue ownership-confirming registry extracts for buildings, non-residential premises, and land plots.
  • Lease & Property Vacating — Drafting of long-term lease agreements for commercial premises. Compulsory eviction of rent-evading tenants via court order and Enforcement Division.

Why US in Commercial Property?

  • Mortgage & Lien Management — 100% legally compliant registration of mortgages/liens at the Central Register when large loans are taken or given, and immediate realisation of collateral if needed.
  • Rapid Intervention When Construction Stops — Swift legal intervention within hours against unlawful decisions to halt works by Urban Planning Committee, MES, or environmental bodies.
  • Identification of True Rights Holders — Pre-transaction identification of risks relating to land with complex ownership and inheritance disputes buried in state archives.

Frequently Asked Questions on Real Estate

A due diligence audit checks the legality of the property's documents, third-party rights, court disputes, tax encumbrances, and hidden debts, freezes, and undischarged mortgages in the State Register before purchase.

When the MES, State Urban Planning Committee, or local executive authorities unlawfully delay a construction permit, we immediately initiate a court dispute and ensure the permit is formalised by court order.

When the State Registry of Real Estate refuses to issue a title deed, an administrative complaint must be filed. The problem is resolved by having the registration body bound by obligation through administrative courts.

When acceptance of completed works under Form-2 and Form-3 acts is refused or payment is delayed, we file claims in commercial courts. The dispute is resolved through an independent judicial construction-technical expert assessment.

Azerbaijani civil legislation strongly protects property owners. When rent is unpaid (for 2 consecutive months or as stated in the contract), a claim is filed for contract termination, recovery of rent arrears and penalty interest, and compulsory eviction of the tenant.
