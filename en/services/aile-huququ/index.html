Family Law

Family matters are not only a legal process but also an emotional one. The PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC's family law team protects your rights in sensitive areas such as divorce, property division, child welfare, and alimony claims with full confidentiality and professional support.

Family Law in Baku: Divorce, Property Division & Children's Rights. Professional Legal Support

Family disputes are one of the most sensitive periods in a person's life. The PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC family law team provides professional legal support in full confidentiality in the areas of divorce proceedings, division of marital property, protection of children's rights, alimony, and family mediation. Applying an individual approach, we protect our clients' rights in accordance with the Family Code of Azerbaijan.

Whether it is a consensual divorce, a disputed property division, or the regulation of child contact arrangements, our team is by your side at every step. Initial consultations are conducted in full confidentiality.

What Services Do We Provide in Family Disputes?

  • Divorce Case Representation — full legal representation in consensual and contested divorce cases, preparation of applications, and court defence
  • Property Division — lawful division of movable and immovable property acquired during marriage; resolution of ownership disputes
  • Protection of Children's Rights — determination of a child's place of residence, contact arrangements, restriction or restoration of parental rights
  • Alimony Enforcement & Increase — determination of alimony amount, indexation, preparation of enforcement documents, and recovery from the debtor
  • Family Mediation — professional mediation service to help parties reach an out-of-court agreement
  • Residential Registration & Deregistration — registration at a place of residence, deregistration, and resolution of related disputes
  • Prenuptial Agreements — drafting of agreements governing property relations before and after marriage

Most Frequently Asked Questions on Family Law

A consensual divorce typically concludes within 1–2 months. In contested cases involving property division, children's rights, or alimony, the process may take 3–12 months. The PROBIZ LEGAL HUB team provides strategic legal support to accelerate the process.

Under the Family Code, property acquired during marriage is divided equally. Pre-marital property and property acquired by inheritance or as a gift are considered personal property. In disputed cases, the court makes a decision taking the specific circumstances into account.

The court takes the child's interests as the primary consideration: age, health, relationship with parents, and material provision are assessed. When parents cannot agree, the court determines the child's place of residence.

Alimony is determined as a percentage of the parent's income: one-quarter (25%) for one child, one-third (33%) for two children, one-half (50%) for three or more. Alimony can also be set as a fixed monetary amount.

Family mediation is the process of parties reaching an agreement through out-of-court discussion. In Azerbaijan, participation in a mediation session before going to court is mandatory. For children's rights, property division, and alimony matters, mediation can be more efficient in terms of both time and cost.
