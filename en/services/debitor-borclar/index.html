Debt Recovery

Unpaid debts should not hinder your business's growth. At PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC, using legal claims, court proceedings, and enforcement stages, we ensure that even the most complex and problematic debtor debts are lawfully recovered and credited to your company's accounts.

Recovery of Debts from Individuals and Legal Entities in Baku

Partners who dodge their obligations and debtors who buy time with empty promises are business's greatest vulnerability. Our experience shows: the only way to break that pattern is sustained, unrelenting legal pressure. We treat your unpaid debt not as a number to write off, but as a company asset worth fighting for.

Our approach is not based on a wait-and-hope strategy. The PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC team resolutely defends cases involving underpayments, unsecured credits, and unrecovered advances, both at the pre-litigation table and in court and enforcement stages, ensuring funds are physically transferred to your company's account.

How Do We Pursue Unpaid Debts?

  • Pre-Litigation Resolution (Mediation & Demand) — Sending legally effective demand letters to debtors to avoid court costs; compelling the debtor towards compromise by explaining their legal liabilities and the real risk of account freezes.
  • Asset Identification — Pre-litigation identification of vehicles, real estate, bank accounts, or shares in the debtor's name to confirm whether a real source of repayment exists.
  • Commercial Court Representation — Filing claims against those who refuse to pay voluntarily; building the full evidentiary case and arguing it in court.
  • Preventive Measures (Asset Seizure) — Obtaining pre-trial asset freezes on the debtor's accounts to prevent them from transferring or hiding funds before the case concludes.
  • Full Enforcement of Court Judgments — Working alongside enforcement officers to ensure that funds are debited from the debtor's accounts or their assets are auctioned.
  • Encashment of Bills & Promissory Notes — Rapid encashment procedure for debt instruments issued notarially or in simple written form.

Why PROBIZ LEGAL HUB LLC for Debt Recovery?

  • Uncompromising Legal Pressure — Our lawyers are not fooled by debtors' delay tactics. Through continuous lawful measures, we leave the debtor with only one choice: pay.
  • Our Strength in Enforcement — Many lawyers leave matters at the court judgment stage. We continue intensive monitoring through enforcement until funds are directly credited to your account.
  • Speed & Results-Focus — In debt matters, the longer it takes, the more value is lost. We apply the most rigorous yet fully lawful instruments from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions on Debt Recovery

Against debtors who show no response to official warnings, a claim is immediately filed in commercial or civil court. In parallel, a petition is submitted to freeze the debtor's bank accounts and assets, so they cannot hide their funds.

Compulsory bank account debiting or asset confiscation can only be carried out by enforcement officers on the basis of a court judgment in force. However, thanks to our firm demand service, most debts are recovered without going to court through formal legal pressure.

Cases resolved at the demand stage conclude within 15–30 days. If the matter goes to court, it can take 2 to 6 months depending on complexity. The most complex stage is enforcement, where we conduct continuous monitoring with enforcement officers.

In such cases, we investigate whether the debtor has intentionally hidden assets or transferred them to relatives or other companies (fraudulent transfer). If intentional evasion is detected, this creates criminal liability and becomes a powerful legal tool for recovery.

Resolving the matter without going to court is the most cost-effective option. A demand letter by a strong lawyer shows the debtor the seriousness of the situation, large expected court costs, and reputational damage, in most cases forcing an immediate compromise.
